Who We Are

A space that is truly ours

Actually Autistic SD is a peer-led community for autistic adults in San Diego. Grassroots, independent, and completely community driven. Built together, led with care.

We are not a professional organization, a clinical service, or affiliated with any autism nonprofit. We are simply autistic people who wanted a space that truly belongs to us.

Founded in the spring of 2026, we are a growing and evolving community. We are just getting started and we are so glad you are here. 💜

Our Story

Why I created this

Welcome to Actually Autistic SD 💜♾️

After so much love and encouragement from my autistic peers, I built the space they kept telling me I was meant to create. My friends believed in me before I fully believed in myself. They kept saying "you should just do it" and eventually I listened.

This is a peer-led community for autistic adults in San Diego. Grassroots, independent and completely community driven. Built together, led with care. I bring warmth, deep attention to detail, and a genuine passion for making sure every person here feels seen and safe. Autistic voices are the foundation of everything we do here, and as founder and organizer I make sure that safety, respect, and accountability are always the priority — because that matters deeply to us.

Community Guidelines

Our shared values

By participating in Actually Autistic SD, online or in person, you agree to these guidelines. They exist to keep our space safe, warm, and truly ours.

💜

1. This is a Peer Support Space

Actually Autistic SD is a peer-led community run by and for autistic adults. We are not a licensed or professional service of any kind. We do not provide therapy, diagnosis, or clinical support. Nothing shared here should be taken as medical, legal, or mental health advice.

🌈

2. Autistic Identity

This space is for autistic adults. Formally diagnosed, self-diagnosed, or questioning. All are welcome. We do not require proof of diagnosis and we do not gatekeep autistic identity.

🛡️

3. Safety & Respect — Zero Tolerance

We have a zero tolerance policy for:

  • Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind
  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or ableism
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate behavior
  • Sharing anyone's personal information without consent
  • Screenshots or sharing anything outside the group

Violations will result in immediate removal from the group.

📵

4. Privacy & Confidentiality

What is shared here stays here. Please respect the privacy of every member. Do not share names, photos, personal details or anything discussed in this space with anyone outside of it. No screenshots — ever.

🧠

5. Mental Health & Crisis

We care deeply about every member of this community. However we are not equipped to provide crisis support. If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health emergency please reach out to:

  • 🚨 988 — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text)
  • 🚨 911 — Emergency services
  • 🏥 Your nearest emergency room
📍

6. In-Person Meetups

All IRL meetups are held in public spaces for the safety of all members. Actually Autistic SD is not responsible for any incidents that occur before, during, or after meetups. Attendance is voluntary and at your own risk.

💬

7. Communication & Tone

We are all here because we get it. Please extend the same grace to others that you would want extended to you. This is a judgment free zone. Healthy disagreement is welcome, but please keep all communication kind and respectful.

🚫

8. No Soliciting

Please do not use this space to promote, sell, or advertise products, services, or other groups without prior approval from the founder and organizer.

📝

9. Admin & Moderation

Actually Autistic SD is founded and organized by an autistic adult in San Diego. As organizer I have the final say on all matters related to safety, membership and community decisions. Depending on the situation, I will consult other admins, organizers and/or members. This is to ensure the safety and wellbeing of all members always.

📋

10. Disclaimer

Actually Autistic SD is a peer support community and is not a licensed or professional service of any kind. We do not provide therapy, diagnosis or clinical support of any kind. Participation is voluntary and at your own risk.

🔍

11. Member Authenticity & Vetting

Actually Autistic SD is a real, peer-led community built on genuine human connection. While we deeply respect every member's privacy and right to share only what they feel comfortable sharing, AI-generated profiles, fake identities, and bot accounts will not be tolerated.

Members are welcome to use nicknames or go by a name they feel comfortable with. However, as founder and organizer, I must be able to verify that every member is a real person in order to maintain the safety and integrity of our community. This is not about surveillance. It is about ensuring that every person in this space is who they say they are, so that all of our members can feel genuinely safe.

If there is ever reason to question the authenticity of a member, the organizer reserves the right to ask clarifying questions or remove the account from the community. This will always be handled with care and discretion.

Our community is by us and for us and that means real autistic humans only. 💜

These guidelines may be updated at any time as our community grows and evolves. While we will do our best to notify members of any changes via the WhatsApp Announcements channel, it is ultimately each member's responsibility to review these guidelines regularly. Actually Autistic SD is not responsible if a member misses an update. Your continued participation means you agree to the most current version of these guidelines — so please check back often! 💜

Thank you for helping make this space truly ours 💜♾️

By autistics, for autistics. Always. 🌈
